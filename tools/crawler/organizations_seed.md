# Organizations Seed (US Aikido)

Seed list of US aikido organizations for the crawler's Phase A (organizations). Maps to the
`organization` schema; `lineage` is one of: aikikai | ki-society | yoshinkan | tomiki |
independent.

Verify every URL in Phase A against the org's own site before relying on it. URLs marked
(confirm) were not verified live and may be wrong; resolve them first. "All" is aspirational
-- a long tail of single-dojo and small regional bodies is not captured here.
